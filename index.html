Step 0: Reset stored values
If you used this calculator before and the values it stored for you are no longer valid, clear them with this button.

Step 1: How often can you/do you want to check your phone?
0 hours 0 minutes means you can check it immediately when a logistic finishes.
If you are going to sleep, put down however long you sleep for unless you check your phone in your sleep.

Presets:

Every hours and minutes
Step 2: How long are you running the logistics for?
Typically people are awake 16 hours and asleep 8 hours a day.
You can go beyond 24 hours if you plan on being away for more than a day, for example. But since there is no logistic that goes beyond 24 hours, the results will not change.

Presets:

hours and minutes
Step 3: How much do you care about each of the following resources/items?
0 means you do not care at all. 1 means you care. 2 means you care twice as much. 3 means you care 3 times as much, etc.
